VA Center for Innovation

  • about
  • portfolio
  • competitions
  • partnerships

The VA Center for Innovation identifies, tests, and evaluates new approaches to efficiently and effectively meet the current and future needs of Veterans through innovations rooted in data, design-thinking, and agile development.

We believe:

• Data should drive decisions

• User needs should drive development of products, services, programs... everything

• We must test small, fail small, and scale big

• There is enormous value and insight beyond the walls of the VA Central Office. If we truly want to build a Veteran-centered VA, we must get out of the building and get to know our Veterans and VA employees

Pilot Program for Student Veterans

VACI is sponsoring a project in partnership with the University of Iowa to design and test a comprehensive suite of on-campus services for Veterans attending colleges and universities. The model program is intended to ease the transition from military service to academia and employment. Learn more ›
